RouteFinder.

Description

A route finder simplifies the process of trip planning and navigation, making it easier for individuals and businesses to travel efficiently and reach their destinations with confidence..

Inspiration Behind the Project

Imagine growing up in a picturesque but remote town, surrounded by farms and forests. I used to bike for hours to visit my closest friend in the next town, with cornfields on both sides, the sun rising, and the scent of harvest in the air. Those rides at dawn were etched into my memory. When I moved to the city, biking brought back those memories, and I realized the joy of cycling should be accessible to all.
